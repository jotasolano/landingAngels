RELAX IN THE PACIFIC

Formerly known as “La Norma Guanacaste”, Angels Ecolodge comprises over 9 acres (430 000 sqr. ft.), of Tropical Dry Forest and it’s so much more than just a guest room. Our low impact lodging option is based on sustainability practices that help protect the fragile areas in the surroundings and encourage sustainable economic community development, it is owned and managed locally. It offers turists a different experience, a close up with culture and nature.

ROOMS & RATES

We have 10 extraordinary bungalows that blend in with the nature around our Ecolodge. Here you can enjoy the peaceful and warm atmosphere. Our bungalows are built with materials from the “finca”, keeping peace with the environment.

We offer rooms with special access for people with physical disabilities who have difficulties using stairs. Contact us to know more.
